Mi Galería de Imágenes

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Jimena Mariel S.

Dolphin

They sleep with one eye open and half of their brain inactive.

Turtle

Sea turtles can live between 50 and 100 years.

Conch

Some species of snails can live for decades.

Corals

Marine corals are animals, not plants, despite their appearance as rocks or marine plants.

Octopus

Most octopuses reproduce only once in their lives, and the female dies shortly after the eggs hatch.

Fishes

Fish can make different sounds, such as grunts, moans, or whistles, to communicate with each other.

Jellyfish

Some species are bioluminescent, that is, they can produce their own light.

Whales

Some species of whales, such as the bowhead whale, can live more than 200 years.
